5 Essential Poses for Every Beginner

Lisa Cooper

January 19, 2026 • 7 min read

Stepping onto a yoga mat for the first time can be intimidating. You see people twisting into shapes that seem physically impossible. But remember: every expert was once a beginner. Yoga is not about performance; it’s about exploration. Here are five foundational poses that will help you build strength, balance, and confidence.

1. Mountain Pose (Tadasana)

This is the base for all standing poses. It may look like you're just standing there, but it's an active pose that helps improve posture and body awareness. Focus on grounding through all four corners of your feet.

2. Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)

A classic for a reason! This pose stretches the entire body, from your fingertips to your heels. It builds strength in the arms and shoulders while lengthening the spine.

3. Warrior I & II

These poses build power in the legs and open the hips. They teach you how to stay strong and steady while keeping your upper body relaxed and your breath fluid.
