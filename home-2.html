Our Mission

More Than Just a Studio

At Zenith, we believe yoga is a path to self-discovery. Our mission is to create a space where you can explore your potential, both on and off the mat.

Founded in 2010, we have grown from a small community center to a hub of wellness, offering over 50 classes a week ranging from Hatha to Aerial Yoga.
